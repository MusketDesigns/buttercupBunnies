My Beloved Buttercup

Buttercup Bunnies was founded in June 2017. I have been crocheting since
a young age and was inspired to make bunnies because of my love for all
my real bunnies.

My first bunny, Buttercup, hopped into my life in 2014 and she was the most
loveable and regal bunny with white fur, pink nose and beautiful blue eyes.
Sadly, she crossed the rainbow bridge in April 2022.

In honour of her, I want to share my love of bunnies and crocheting with others.
All my Buttercup bunnies and friends are made with love and care.
- Belinda Wessels, Founder and Creator
